I am a third year PhD student in Department of Statistics, the Ohio State University. Before coming to OSU, I got my bachelor degrees in mathematics and economics from Wuhan University, China, in 2017. I am now working under the supervision of Professor Yunzhang Zhu. My research interests span statistical learning, computational statistics, optimization and their applications in computer vision. Some of the codes from my research are available on GitHub.
